About King-David Autos

Meet Our CEO

Deajir Tordue King-David

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

With over a decade of experience in the automotive industry, Deajir Tordue King-David founded King-David Autos with a vision to revolutionize the car buying experience in Makurdi, Benue State.

His commitment to excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction has made King-David Autos a trusted name in the automotive sector. Under his leadership, the company has grown to become one of the premier car dealerships in the region.

Deajir believes that every customer deserves personalized attention and the highest quality vehicles. His hands-on approach ensures that every car in our inventory meets the strictest quality standards.

When he's not at the dealership, Deajir enjoys spending time with his family and contributing to community development initiatives in Benue State.

Our Story

King-David Autos was established with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide the people of Makurdi and surrounding areas with access to high-quality vehicles at fair prices, backed by exceptional customer service.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a trusted automotive destination, known for our integrity, transparency, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Every vehicle we sell comes with our guarantee of quality and reliability.

Located in the heart of Makurdi, Benue State, we serve customers from across the region, providing them with carefully selected vehicles that meet their needs and exceed their expectations.

Our Values

Integrity

We conduct all our business with honesty and transparency. Our customers can trust that we provide accurate information about every vehicle and fair pricing without hidden fees.

Quality

Every vehicle in our inventory undergoes rigorous inspection to ensure it meets our high standards. We only offer cars that we would be proud to drive ourselves.

Customer Service

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We provide personalized service and ongoing support to ensure your complete satisfaction with your purchase.

Our Commitment

At King-David Autos, we are committed to making your car buying experience enjoyable and stress-free. We understand that purchasing a vehicle is a significant investment, and we are here to guide you every step of the way.
